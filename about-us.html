About Us

At 800 Car Storage, we provide a secure and refined environment designed exclusively for the protection of luxury, sports, and premium vehicles. Every detail of our facility is built to deliver peace of mind, discretion, and uncompromising standards of care. Our climate-controlled indoor storage spaces are monitored around the clock, ensuring your vehicle remains protected from environmental damage, unauthorized access, and long-term wear. Whether you require short-term storage or an extended solution, we offer reliability, precision, and professionalism at every step. From controlled access and 24/7 surveillance to dedicated vehicle handling and maintenance-ready storage conditions, 800 Car Storage is where your car is preserved—not just parked. .

Why Choose Us

At 800 Car Storage , we go beyond secure parking to deliver a complete vehicle care experience. Every aspect of our facility is designed to protect, preserve, and professionally manage your vehicle—so you can enjoy total peace of mind.
